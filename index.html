Personal Profile

I am a statistician in the Medical Affairs group at Astellas Pharma. In addition, I am currently working on my PhD in the Statistics and Probability group at the University of York. My PhD research is looking at applying wavelet methods to problems in medical statistics. Previously, I was a senior statistical analyst at OPEN Health, working in the Modelling & Meta-Analysis department. I have several years of experience of clinical trial analysis and health technology assessment procedures. I started at OPEN Health after graduating with an MMath in Mathematics from the University of York. I also have an MSc in Statistics with Medical Applications from the University of Sheffield.
