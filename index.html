Personal Profile

I am a statistician at Astellas Pharma, working on oncology trials. Previously, I was a senior statistical analyst at OPEN Health, working in the Modelling & Meta-Analysis department. I started at OPEN Health straight after graduating with an MMath in Mathematics from the University of York. I also have an MSc in Statistics with Medical Applications from the University of Sheffield.
